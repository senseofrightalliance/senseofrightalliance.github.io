Sense Of Right Alliance

The SORA is an alliance of unexplainable beings that have come together for good. Our mission is to document the nature of current members, potential members, and all other beings with mysterious properties. By doing this, we improve our members' abilities to counter any beings who try to bring harm to the mortal world.

Most beings are extremely hard to capture digital records of due to puzzling phenomena that interferes with digital technology.
